Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to connect the Cumberland Gap Chamber of Commerce to their community to promote outreach and community involvment. The goal of the Chamber of Commerce is to improve both tourist and community traffic through our town and promote the growth of our local businesses. Keep the Gap Local.
