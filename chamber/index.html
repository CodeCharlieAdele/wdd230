Welcome to the town of Cumberland Gap

Immerse Yourself in Nature: Escape the hustle and bustle of the city and breathe in the crisp, clean air of our picturesque small town. Surrounded by breathtaking landscapes, our town offers hiking trails, serene parks, and stunning vistas that will leave you in awe. Shop Local, Support Local: Experience the warmth of our tight-knit community by exploring our unique boutiques, charming craft stores, and delightful artisanal shops. From handmade treasures to locally sourced products, our small town is a treasure trove of one-of-a-kind finds. Savor Local Flavors: Indulge your taste buds with the flavors of our local cuisine. From cozy cafes serving freshly baked pastries to family-owned restaurants offering farm-to-table dishes, our town is a culinary haven that will tantalize your senses. Celebrate Community Spirit: Join us for vibrant community events that bring neighbors, families, and friends together. From lively street festivals and farmers' markets to art exhibitions and live performances, our small town is alive with the spirit of togetherness.
