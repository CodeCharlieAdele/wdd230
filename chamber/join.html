Cumberland Gap Chamber Membership Level Information & Join Form

NP Membership

◼ All the benefits of enhanced membership, plus:

◼ Invitations to exclusive leadership forums and roundtable discussions.

◼ VIP access to high-profile chamber events and guest speakers.

◼ Opportunities to serve on the chamber's board of directors or executive committees.

Bronze Membership

◼ Access to networking events and business mixers.

◼ Listing in the chamber's online directory.

◼ Subscription to the chamber's newsletter or publications.

◼ Opportunities to participate in chamber committees and task forces.

◼ Access to educational resources and workshops.

Silver Membership

◼ All the benefits of basic membership, plus:

◼ Increased visibility and recognition, such as a highlighted listing in the directory or additional promotional opportunities.

◼ Priority referrals and leads from the chamber.

◼ Additional advertising and marketing opportunities.

◼ Enhanced advocacy and representation for the member's interests.

Gold Membership

◼ All the benefits of enhanced membership, plus:

◼ Invitations to exclusive leadership forums and roundtable discussions.

◼ VIP access to high-profile chamber events and guest speakers.

◼ Opportunities to serve on the chamber's board of directors or executive committees.

◼ Customized sponsorship packages for major chamber events.
